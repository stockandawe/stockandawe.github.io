Reverse salient

Find the "reverse salient"

An idea from the history of science to generate startup ideas or ways to help in a crisis: find the reverse salient.

A reverse salient is the technology or process that is holding back development of the whole system (like ⚡️car batteries did). Solving the salient unlocks change
