Focus

The great warrior was an average warrior with laser-like focus.
"If it's not a hell yes, it's a no"
- Shaan Puri
"You can do anything, but you can't do everything"
- Shaan Puri
"The main thing is to keep the main thing the main thing."
- Kevin Kelly
Focus is not just the ability to say no to many things. It's the ability to say no to even things that with every bone in your body you believe is a phenomenal idea, and you wake up thinking about it, but you say no to because you are thinking about something else.
