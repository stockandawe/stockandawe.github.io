Rutul Davè

Builds products & startups.

Rutul is a entrepreneur with a background in creating products and building startups.

In 2012, he founded Bright Funds with a vision to reimagine charitable giving, and build a platform for a new generation of donors focused on impact. With three startups and an exit under his belt, he lives and breathes startups.

As an active voice on immigration reform, he was featured on The Wall Street Journal in 2013 and on CauseArtist’s list of social entrepreneurs to watch for in 2014.
